SINCLAIR GALA

Sinclair Global launched a new collection on September 22 in celebration of its one-year anniversary, which highlights best-selling products in reimagined, limited-edition colorways. Founded during the COVID-19 pandemic by former hedge fund trader and VIANEL founder Andrew Books, the brand aims to bring fresh streetwear-focused garments to the inner cities. To showcase the new collection, Sinclair Global joined forces with Detroit-rapper and actor, Demetrius “Lil’ Meech” Flenory Jr., known for his role in the upcoming STARZ drama series, “BMF.” The anniversary collection features the brand’s Clair Bear t-shirt in white, the Gala hoodie and matching sweatpants in grey, a navy sweatsuit with a jockey-inspired graphic and a black sweatsuit with an orange Sinclair Global graphic. Other offerings include a navy fleece jacket, a sherpa quarter-zip in beige with orange branding and the brand’s signature hats.
